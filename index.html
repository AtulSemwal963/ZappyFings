Games that get you zapping your fingers
Clicks Per Second Test
Clicks Per Second Test is a motor ability test that requires you to click on a specified area as many times as you can for a certain period of time. It analyzes the ability of your muscles to not only work at a fast rate but also to maintain the same pace over a duration of time. But be sure not to push yourself too hard as excessively working the same muscle can cause severe strains to the finger, wrists, or the entire foremarm as well.
Defuse The C4
Defuse The C4 is a quick little memorize and type game where a 4 character code will be flashed onto the screen for 2 seconds before the timer on the C4 starts counting down. You will have few seconds to enter the code before the C4 goes off, resulting in an explosion, and ending the game. You will be awarded with points after defusing the C4. Play along with your friends and see who racks up the highest score.
